1. Introduction

Welcome to **Japanese Anki Flashcards** ("the app"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you provide while using the app. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect data.

2. Data Collection

The app **does not collect any personal data** from users. Specifically:

  • No login information, email, or name is collected.
  • No location tracking.
  • No cookies or third-party analytics services.
  • No study data is sent to external servers.

All study data entered by the user (e.g., vocabulary you add, learning progress, quiz results) is **stored locally on your device** and is never transmitted to any server.

3. Data Usage

Since the app does not collect personal data, we do not use or process any personal data on external servers.

4. Data Sharing

We **do not share** user data with any third parties, including advertisers, analytics providers, or other partners.

5. Data Stored on Device

All information entered by the user is stored locally on the device. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, this information will be deleted from the device.

6. User Rights

Since no data is sent to our servers, you do not need to request access or deletion from us. To erase all study data, simply uninstall the app or use the in-app data-clearing feature (if available).

7. Security

Because the app does not transmit data externally, there is *no risk* of data leaks from our servers. However, you should secure your device (password, screen lock) to protect locally stored data.

8. Contact

If you have any questions or privacy-related requests, please contact us via email: **support@japaneseankiflashcards.com**.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL and take effect upon posting. Please check this page regularly for the latest version.
